My Photography Journey

What is Photography?

Photography is the art and practice of capturing light to create images. It has been my greatest passion for the past five years. I love exploring different techniques and discovering new perspectives through my camera lens. Some gear is essential for quality results.

Essential Equipment

  • Digital Camera
    • DSLR cameras
    • Mirrorless cameras
    • Smartphone cameras
  • Lenses (wide-angle, telephoto, macro)
  • Tripod and stabilizers
  • Lighting equipment

My Photography Journey Steps

  1. Learned the basics of composition and lighting
  2. Invested in my first DSLR camera
  3. Practiced portrait and landscape photography
  4. Started a portfolio online
  5. Began taking client projects
