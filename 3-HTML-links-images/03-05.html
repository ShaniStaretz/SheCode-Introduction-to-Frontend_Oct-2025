Grumpy Cat

Grumpy Cat, real name Tardar Sauce, was an American cat and internet celebrity known for her grumpy facial expression. She was born on April 4, 2012, and passed away on May 14, 2019. Grumpy Cat's unique appearance was due to feline dwarfism and an underbite. Her iconic design made her stand out online, and you can read more about her on Wikipedia. She became an internet sensation after a photo of her was posted on Reddit in September 2012, leading to widespread popularity and numerous memes.
