My Photography Journey

What is Photography?

from CHAT GTP:

Photography as a hobby is the practice of taking pictures for enjoyment and creative expression, using a camera or phone to capture moments, scenes, or ideas.

Photography is the art and practice of capturing light to create images. It has been my greatest passion for the past five years. I love exploring different techniques and discovering new perspectives through my camera lens.
