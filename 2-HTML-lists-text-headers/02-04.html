Favorite Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:

1 cup all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
1 egg
2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
2.Whisk milk, egg, and melted butter together.
3.Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until combined.
4.Heat a skillet, pour batter, and cook until bubbles form.
5.Flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.
